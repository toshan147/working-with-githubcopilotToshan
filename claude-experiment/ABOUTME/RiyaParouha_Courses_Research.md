## SECTION A: UNIVERSITY POSTGRADUATE PROGRAMMES IN WROCŁAW (Studia Podyplomowe)

### 5. ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants)

**Why ACCA:** Globally recognised accounting qualification. Highly valued by SSCs in Wrocław (UBS, BNY Mellon, HP, etc.)
**Website:** [accaglobal.com](https://www.accaglobal.com) | Poland contact: +48 12 395 70 90

- **Eligibility:** B.Com graduates can register directly. Riya may get exemptions from up to 4 papers (F1–F4) depending on her B.Com curriculum.
- **Structure:** 13 exams total (minus exemptions) across 3 levels: Applied Knowledge → Applied Skills → Strategic Professional
- **Exam sessions:** 4 times per year (March, June, September, December)
- **Study mode:** Self-study or via approved learning partners
- **Duration:** Typically 2–3 years part-time (less with exemptions)
- **Practical requirement:** 36 months of relevant work experience (can be completed while studying)
- **Cost:** Registration fee + per-exam fees (~£100–150 per exam)
- **Exam centres:** Available in Poland (Wrocław/Kraków/Warsaw)
- **RECOMMENDATION:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — The gold standard. Even completing a few ACCA papers (without full qualification) significantly boosts employability. Start with Applied Knowledge level.

## SECTION E: FREE TRAINING VIA URZĄD PRACY (LABOUR OFFICE)

**Website:** [wroclaw.praca.gov.pl](https://wroclaw.praca.gov.pl)
**Address:** Powiatowy Urząd Pracy we Wrocławiu
**Applications:** Room 04, ground floor, Monday–Friday 8:00–15:00

### What's available:
- **Free vocational training** for registered unemployed persons or job seekers
- Training costs covered up to **300% of the average salary**
- Participants receive a **training stipend** during the programme
- Available training areas change based on market demand — typically include: office administration, bookkeeping basics, computer skills, Polish language

### How to access:
1. Riya should **register as a job seeker** (osoba poszukująca pracy) at Urząd Pracy — this does NOT require being unemployed, just seeking work
2. Meet with a career counsellor to discuss training options
3. Apply for individual or group training
4. Decision is made within 30 days

### Requirements:
- Must lack qualifications OR need to change/update qualifications (Riya qualifies given her career gap)
- Valid Karta Pobytu (she has this)

**RECOMMENDATION:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — This should be Riya's FIRST STOP. Completely free, and the career counselling alone is valuable. They may fund a Polish language course, bookkeeping course, or computer skills training at no cost.
