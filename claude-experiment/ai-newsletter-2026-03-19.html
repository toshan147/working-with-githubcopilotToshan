📰 TOP STORIES

🧠 Yann LeCun's AMI Labs Raises Record $1B Seed Round

Backed by Bezos, NVIDIA, Samsung, and Temasek, AMI Labs is building "world models" based on JEPA architecture. LeCun believes true human-level AI requires systems that understand the physical world, not just language.

🤖 NVIDIA Unleashes Physical AI with Cosmos & Isaac

NVIDIA dropped a massive physical AI update: new Cosmos world models, Isaac simulation frameworks, and a Physical AI Data Factory Blueprint. Partners include ABB, Figure, KUKA, and Universal Robots. The goal? Slash training time and cost for intelligent robots by orders of magnitude. The robotics revolution just got its operating system.

💰 AI Funding Hits Record: $189B in February Alone

February 2026 was the biggest single month for startup funding in history — $189 billion globally. March is already on pace to beat it, with $100M+ AI rounds dropping daily. Standouts: Mind Robotics ($500M), Rhoda AI ($450M), and legal AI platform Legora ($550M). Money is pouring into AI faster than ever before.

🔥 TRENDING IN AI

Deepfake Crisis Hits Tipping Point

From political campaign ads featuring fabricated candidates to AI-generated war footage racking up hundreds of millions of views — deepfakes are dominating the conversation. The Stephen Colbert deepfake, fake CEO videos, and misleading Iran-Israel conflict footage have created what experts call a "watershed moment" for trust in online media. The UN and global regulators are flagging AI-powered fraud as an emerging threat. The debate: we've built incredible tools, but who controls the off-switch?

💡 PROMPT OF THE DAY

"I need you to think like a domain expert, then act like a teacher. First, explain [topic] as if you're an authority in the field using 2-3 key insights I might not know.
